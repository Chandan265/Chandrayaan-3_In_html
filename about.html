About Chandrayaan-3 Mission

Chandrayaan-3 is India's latest lunar exploration mission, poised to continue the nation's rich legacy of space exploration and scientific discovery.

Mission Objectives

The primary objectives of Chandrayaan-3 include:

  • Exploration of the lunar surface
  • Collection of high-resolution imagery and geological data
  • Studying lunar topography and mineral composition
  • Advancing our understanding of the moon's history and evolution

Significance

Chandrayaan-3 represents a significant step forward in lunar exploration. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and the expertise of India's space agency, ISRO, this mission aims to contribute valuable insights to the global scientific community.
